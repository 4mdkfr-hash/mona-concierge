## What is MonaConcierge?

MonaConcierge is an AI-powered concierge that handles all your client messages 24/7 — in Russian, French, and English. It answers questions, shares your prices and services, and books appointments automatically, so you never miss a client again.

## How It Works

```
Client sends WhatsApp message
        ↓
MonaConcierge AI responds instantly
(knows your services, prices & schedule)
        ↓
Appointment confirmed automatically
        ↓
You get notified — nothing to do
```

1. **Client writes** to your WhatsApp number at any time
2. **AI responds** immediately with accurate information about your venue
3. **Booking confirmed** — no manual work required
4. **You stay informed** via the dashboard and notifications
