## 4. Social Media & Platform Access

Required for initial setup and ad campaigns.

| Platform | Access type | Status |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Facebook Business Manager | Admin access | ☐ |
| Instagram Business Account | Admin access | ☐ |
| Google Business Profile | Manager access | ☐ |

> **How to grant access:**
> - Facebook/Instagram: Business Settings → Users → Partners → Add partner by Business ID
> - Google Business Profile: Manage Location → Users → Add user by email
